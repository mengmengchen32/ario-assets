None of this is new. The shopping-data industry has run on account linking for years — people connecting their email, retailer, loyalty, or bank accounts to share what they buy.

It isn’t fringe or unusual. It runs at real scale today, marketed in the open — across research incumbents, rewards apps, and household-name brands.
