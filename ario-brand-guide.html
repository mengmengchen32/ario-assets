01 — Logo

Logo

The Ario logo is a custom wordmark with a distinctive open-stroke "a" and two blue dots. The dots are integral to the mark — one floats above-left of the "a", one sits above the junction of "r" and "i". Never alter, recreate, or reinterpret the logo.

Primary
Reversed
On Blue

Clear Space

x x x x

Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "o" on all sides. No other graphic elements should enter this zone.

Minimum Size

Minimum width: 80px for digital, 20mm for print. Below this size the dots lose legibility.

04 — Relationship

Ario + Twin Persona

Two products, one system. Ario provides the data; Twin Persona simulates what consumers will do next. They share a visual language but have distinct identities.

TWIN PERSONA
"Simulate what consumers will do."
Behavioral digital twins built on real purchase data. Agent-based simulation for CPG and retail strategy teams.
"See what consumers actually buy."
Customer-consented, SKU-level purchase data infrastructure. The ground-truth layer that powers everything.

Shared

  • Blue accent (#2B5CFF)
  • Outfit + DM Sans + JetBrains Mono
  • Same grid system and spacing
  • Enterprise-credible, data-forward tone

Different

  • Logo form: uppercase lockup vs. lowercase with dots
  • Tone: forward-looking simulation vs. ground-truth data
  • Audience: strategy teams vs. data teams
  • Positioning: product vs. infrastructure
